About

Hi, I'm Amanda.

I'm the founder of Matty Marketing, a full-service email marketing agency based in Kelowna, BC. I help brands build email and SMS programs that actually drive revenue, not just fill inboxes.

With nearly a decade of experience in marketing and a deep specialization in Klaviyo, I've helped ecommerce and service-based brands turn their email lists into their highest-performing sales channels. I manage everything from strategy and copywriting to design, automation, and reporting.

When I'm not in Klaviyo, I'm building websites and landing pages that convert. I believe great marketing should feel effortless to your customers and drive real, measurable results for your business.
